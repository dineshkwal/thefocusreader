# The Focus Reader

**Read with intention. One line at a time.**

A distraction-free PDF reader that guides your focus through text — word by word, sentence by sentence, or paragraph by paragraph.

[**Try it live**](https://thefocusreader.com) &nbsp;&middot;&nbsp; [Report a Bug](https://github.com/dineshkwal/thefocusreader/issues)

---

</div>

## How It Works

Drop a PDF (or paste text), pick a reading mode, and let the reader guide your eyes through the content. Everything else fades away — just you and the words.

### Reading Modes

| Mode | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| **Word** | Highlights one word at a time — great for speed reading |
| **Sentence** | Focuses a full sentence — balanced and natural |
| **Line** | One line at a time — closest to natural reading |
| **Paragraph** | Highlights entire paragraphs — best for skimming |

### Features

- **Auto-advance** with adjustable speed
- **Keyboard controls** — spacebar to play/pause, arrow keys to navigate
- **Three themes** — Dark, Light, and Sepia
- **Chapter detection** with sidebar navigation
- **Reading time estimates**
- **Progress tracking** across sessions
- **Paste text** directly if you don't have a PDF
- **Zero dependencies** — runs entirely in the browser

## Tech

Single HTML file. No build step, no framework, no backend.

- [pdf.js](https://mozilla.github.io/pdf.js/) for PDF parsing
- Google Fonts (Newsreader, DM Sans, JetBrains Mono)
